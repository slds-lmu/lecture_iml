You are given the bike rental data with the features \texttt{season}, \texttt{temp}, \texttt{hum}, \texttt{windspeed}, and \texttt{days\_since\_2011}. A binary target variable $y$ is created:
\vspace{-0.1cm} 
\begin{itemize}
	\item Class $y$=1: ``high number of bike rentals'' $>70\%$ quantile (i.e., \texttt{cnt} $> 5531$)
	\item Class $y$=0: ``low to medium number of bike rentals'' (i.e., \texttt{cnt} $\leq$ 5531)
\end{itemize}
The following table shows the absolute joint and marginal probabilities of $y$ and \texttt{season}.
\begin{table}[H]
	\centering
	\begin{tabular}{rrrrrr}
		\hline
		& WINTER & SPRING & SUMMER & FALL & $\Sigma$ \\ 
		\hline
		$y$=0 & 174.00 & 111.00 & 98.00 & 128.00 & 511.00 \\ 
		$y$=1 & 7.00 & 73.00 & 90.00 & 50.00 & 220.00 \\ 
		$\Sigma$ & 181.00 & 184.00 & 188.00 & 178.00 & 731.00 \\ 
		\hline
	\end{tabular}
\end{table}

\vspace{-0.3cm}
\begin{enumerate}[a)]
	\item Calculate and interpret the odds of ``high number of bike rentals'' vs. ``low to medium number of bike rentals'' in winter ($\text{odds}_{\text{winter}}$).
	\item Calculate and interpret the odds ratio of high vs. low number of bike rentals when \texttt{season} changes from winter to spring.
	\item Consider the output of a GLM on $y \sim \texttt{season}$:
	
	\begin{table}[H]
		\centering
		\begin{tabular}{rrrr}
			\hline
			& Estimate & Std. Error & Pr($>$$|$z$|$) \\ 
			\hline
			(Intercept) & -3.2131 & 0.3854 & 0.0000 \\ 
			seasonSPRING & 2.7941 & 0.4138 & 0.0000 \\ 
			seasonSUMMER & 3.1280 & 0.4121 & 0.0000 \\ 
			seasonFALL & 2.2731 & 0.4199 & 0.0000 \\ 
			\hline
		\end{tabular}
	\end{table}

	Interpret the $\beta$-estimate for the intercept and seasonSPRING. 
	
	\item Now compare the two coefficients with the ones in the full model: 
		\begin{table}[H]
			\centering
			\begin{tabular}{rrrr}
				\hline
				& Estimate & Std. Error & Pr($>$$|$z$|$) \\ 
				\hline
				(Intercept) & -8.5176 & 1.2066 & 0.0000 \\ 
				seasonSPRING & 1.7427 & 0.5977 & 0.0035 \\ 
				seasonSUMMER & -0.8566 & 0.7660 & 0.2635 \\ 
				seasonFALL & -0.6417 & 0.5543 & 0.2470 \\ 
				temp & 0.2902 & 0.0391 & 0.0000 \\ 
				hum & -0.0627 & 0.0124 & 0.0000 \\ 
				windspeed & -0.0925 & 0.0305 & 0.0024 \\ 
				days\_since\_2011 & 0.0166 & 0.0014 & 0.0000 \\ 
				\hline
			\end{tabular}
		\end{table}
\end{enumerate}
